# Diet Plan Requirements - Family of 3

## Overview
- **Number of People:** 3
- **Cooking Setup:** Single person cooks for all
- **Requirements:** Easy-to-cook meals, family meal alignment where possible
- **Location:** Punjab, India
- **Diet Type:** Vegetarian + Eggs (Eggetarian)

---

## Person 1: Young Male - Hypertrophy & Cut

### Basic Information
- **Age:** 22 years
- **Gender:** Male
- **Height:** 169 cm
- **Current Weight:** 71 kg
- **Activity Level:** Gym + 6k steps daily

### Goals
- **Primary Goal:** Create aesthetic body with muscle definition
- **Weight Loss Target:** 0.5 kg per week (cutting phase)
- **Training Focus:** Hypertrophy training

### Caloric & Nutritional Needs
- **BMR (Basic Metabolic Rate):** ~1650 kcal
- **Activity Level:** Moderate-High (gym + daily steps)
- **Estimated TDEE:** ~2400-2500 kcal
- **Target Calories:** ~2000-2100 kcal (deficit of 400-500 kcal for 0.5kg/week loss)
- **Protein Target:** 140-155g daily (2g per kg body weight)
- **Carbs:** Moderate (around workout timing)
- **Fats:** Moderate-High (hormone support)

### Special Considerations
- Pre and post-workout nutrition important
- High protein requirements for muscle maintenance during cut
- Nutrient timing around training sessions

---

## Person 2: Middle-Aged Female - Health & Longevity

### Basic Information
- **Age:** 46 years
- **Gender:** Female
- **Height:** 152 cm
- **Current Weight:** 55 kg
- **Activity Level:** Very active (10k-12k steps daily)

### Goals
- **Primary Goal:** Health optimization and longevity
- **Focus:** Overall wellness, energy, healthy aging

### Medical History
- **Goiter:** Present (without other thyroid issues)
- **Thyroid Function:** Normal (TSH, T3, T4 presumably normal)

### Caloric & Nutritional Needs
- **BMR:** ~1200-1250 kcal
- **Activity Level:** High (10k-12k steps)
- **Estimated TDEE:** ~1800-1900 kcal
- **Target Calories:** ~1700-1800 kcal (slight deficit for health optimization)
- **Protein Target:** 65-75g daily
- **Focus:** Anti-inflammatory foods, antioxidants, micronutrient density

### Special Considerations
- **Iodine Management:** Careful with iodine intake due to goiter
  - May need to moderate iodized salt
  - Monitor iodine-rich foods
- **Bone Health:** Calcium and Vitamin D important at this age
- **Hormonal Balance:** Perimenopause/menopause considerations
- **Anti-inflammatory Diet:** Focus on longevity

---

## Person 3: Middle-Aged Male - Heart Health & Fat Loss

### Basic Information
- **Age:** 50 years
- **Gender:** Male
- **Height:** 175 cm
- **Current Weight:** 75 kg
- **Body Composition:** High visceral fat (big belly)
- **Activity Level:** Not specified (assumed sedentary to light)

### Goals
- **Primary Goals:**
  1. Reduce belly fat (visceral fat)
  2. Gain muscle mass
  3. Lower cholesterol levels
  4. Lower triglycerides
  5. Improve blood pressure

### Medical Conditions
- **High Cholesterol:** Active concern
- **High Blood Pressure:** Active concern
- **Visceral Adiposity:** Central obesity

### Caloric & Nutritional Needs
- **BMR:** ~1550-1600 kcal
- **Activity Level:** Light (need to increase)
- **Estimated TDEE:** ~1900-2000 kcal
- **Target Calories:** ~1600-1700 kcal (moderate deficit)
- **Protein Target:** 90-100g daily (muscle preservation)
- **Carbs:** Lower, focus on complex carbs
- **Fats:** Moderate, emphasis on omega-3s and healthy fats

### Special Considerations
- **Heart Health Priority:**
  - Low sodium intake
  - High fiber (soluble fiber for cholesterol)
  - Omega-3 rich foods
  - Limited saturated fats
  - No trans fats
  - Plant sterols/stanols if possible

- **Blood Pressure Management:**
  - DASH diet principles
  - Potassium-rich foods
  - Magnesium-rich foods
  - Limited sodium (max 1500-2000mg daily)

- **Cholesterol Management:**
  - High fiber intake (especially soluble fiber)
  - Omega-3 fatty acids
  - Plant-based proteins
  - Limit egg yolks (maybe 2-3 per week)
  - Oats, barley, legumes

- **Triglyceride Management:**
  - Limited refined carbs and sugars
  - Omega-3 supplementation
  - Regular meal timing
  - No alcohol
