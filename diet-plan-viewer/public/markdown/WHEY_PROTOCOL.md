## ?? Mixing Instructions

### For Best Results:

1. **Order Matters:**
   - Add liquid first (water/milk)
   - Then add whey powder
   - This prevents clumping

2. **Mixing Methods:**
   - **Best:** Shaker bottle with wire ball (20-30 shakes)
   - **Good:** Blender (10 seconds)
   - **Ok:** Spoon mixing (vigorous, 1 minute)

3. **Temperature:**
   - Cold liquid tastes best
   - Don't use hot liquid (denatures protein)
   - Ice cubes ok for Person 1

4. **Timing:**
   - Mix immediately before drinking
   - Consume within 20 minutes
   - Don't pre-mix and store

## ?? Important Notes

### Storage
- Keep in cool, dry place
- Close lid tightly after each use
- Don't store in refrigerator (moisture risk)
- Use within 6-12 months of opening

### Digestion
- Start with half servings if not used to whey
- Person 1 already uses 2 scoops - no adjustment needed
- Person 2 & 3 starting with 0.5 scoop - monitor digestion
- If gas/bloating occurs, try whey isolate (lower lactose)

### Hydration
- Increase water intake by 500ml when using whey
- Especially important for Person 1 (2 scoops)

### Medical Considerations
- **Person 3 (cholesterol/BP):** Whey may actually help reduce cholesterol
- **Person 2 (goiter):** Whey is fine, low iodine content
- All three: Monitor kidney function (though 72g extra protein is safe)
