### What is Protein Bioavailability?

## üîß STRATEGIES TO IMPROVE PLANT PROTEIN ABSORPTION

### 1. **Sprouting Legumes**
- Increases bioavailability from 65% ‚Üí 75-80%
- Reduces antinutrients (phytates, lectins)
- **Action:** Use sprouts 2-3x per week

### 2. **Combine Incomplete Proteins**
- Rice + Dal = Complete protein (complementary amino acids)
- Wheat + Chickpeas = Complete protein
- **Action:** Already doing this with khichdi!

### 3. **Add Vitamin C to Meals**
- Enhances iron and protein absorption from plant sources
- **Action:** Lemon juice on salads, citrus fruits with meals

### 4. **Reduce Phytates (Antinutrients)**
- Soak dals overnight before cooking
- Ferment grains (e.g., idli/dosa batter)
- **Action:** Soak rajma, chana for 8-12 hours

### 5. **Cook Legumes Properly**
- Pressure cooking reduces antinutrients
- Adds turmeric (enhances absorption)
- **Action:** Already doing this!

### 6. **Distribute Protein Throughout Day**
- Body can only absorb ~30-40g protein per meal optimally
- **Action:** 6 meals per day strategy (already implemented)

### 7. **Pair with Healthy Fats**
- Helps with fat-soluble vitamin absorption (A, D, E, K)
- Slows digestion for better amino acid uptake
- **Action:** Ghee, nuts, olive oil in meals
