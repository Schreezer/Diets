2. **Batch Cooking**: Make sambar and chutney in larger quantities, store 2 days
3. **Jowar Rotis**: Keep dough warm, roll thin, cook immediately (doesn't store well)
4. **Palak Paneer**: Can be made ahead, reheats well
5. **Nut Prep**: Pre-soak almonds/walnuts at night for morning use
6. **Hydration**: Keep water bottles visible, set reminders
7. **Green Tea**: Brew fresh, don't over-steep (becomes bitter)
8. **Whey Timing**: Mix fresh before consuming
